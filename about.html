About me

My name is Liv and I am a self-proclaimed cartoonist from Slovakia. I am about to finish high school and afterwards I would like to pursue a career in the field of animation. However, my greatest passion are comics and their creation. I am currently trying to show my work to the world, so I will do everything I possibly can to get out there, keep on doing what I love and, hopefully, make a living out of it one day.
